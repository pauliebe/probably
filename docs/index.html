The Library of Congress is the world's largest library. In it, I found this photo of Michigan.

Well, it's probably Michigan, according to the title. I like this picture and title combination because it feels like something you would pull out of an old shoebox full of photos, which is not what you generally go to a library for. This is an experimental Library of Congress shoebox, filled with pictures that have probably, maybe, or perhaps in the title.
